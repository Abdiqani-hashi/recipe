  • One Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice

INGREDIENTS

  1. 1 tsp dried oregano
  2. 1 tsp dried thyme
  3. 2 Tbsp cooking oil, divided
  4. 1 cup long-grain white rice (uncooked)
  5. 1.75 cups vegetable broth
  6. Tbsp chopped parsley (optional garnish)
